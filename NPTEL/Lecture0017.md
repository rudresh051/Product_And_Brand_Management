# Lecture 17: Market and Product Planning- 1

## Market Planning

* A marketing plan is a **written** document containing the guidelines for the **business
center's** marketing programs and allocations over the **planning period.**

**Objective of Marketing Plan:**  
1. To define the **current situation (Stage (PLC Point)), Customer Positioning,
Internal-Organizationa/ and Value Chain Positioning, Competitor Positioning**
facing the product (and how we got there).  
2. To define problems and opportunities facing the business. Let's say EV Business
**(Assessment through the eyes of all stakeholders).** This can be Longitudinal.  
3. To establish objectives.
4. To define the strategies and programs necessary to achieve the objectives
**(STEPS).**
5. To pinpoint responsibility for achieving product objectives **(TEAM).**
6. To encourage careful and disciplined thinking **(Method, Training and Execution).**
7. To establish a customer—competitor orientation **(Message).**
